### 1. Product Overview & Key Features

This rubber is the top-spec product actually used by Brazilian table tennis star **Hugo Calderano** on both his forehand and backhand.

* **Product Name:** [Used by Hugo] Jekyll & Hyde C57.5
* **Hardness:** **57.5¬∞** (Very hard, high-density sponge)
* **Key Technologies:**
* **Sticky Top Sheet:** Combines the tackiness (stickiness) of Chinese rubbers with the high repulsion (speed) of tension rubbers.
* **X-Factor Technology:** Features dual-tension technology developed via AI to provide powerful repulsion without the need for separate tuning (boosting).
* **Epsilon Sponge:** Small, uniformly placed pores transfer energy without loss, enabling powerful shots.

| Category | Detailed Evaluation |

### 4. Recommended For

This rubber is **recommended for advanced players rather than beginners or intermediates.**

* Topspin (Drive) style players seeking **powerful knockout shots**.
* Players who previously used **Butterfly Dignics 09C** or **Hurricane 3 (Boosted)** but are looking for stronger repulsion or easier maintenance.
* Players with fast swing speeds and solid impact who can handle a hard rubber.
