### 1. Product Description

The **Jekyll & Hyde** series is a concept rubber designed to challenge the conventional wisdom that opposing properties (Speed vs. Control, Spin vs. Power) cannot coexist, aiming to implement both elements at a top-tier level simultaneously. Among them, the **X Series** focuses specifically on **'Power' and 'Catapult'.**

* **Key Technology (Power & Catapult):**
* **X (E-X-plosion):** The X47.5 applies a **'Sling' mechanism** where the rubber holds the ball deep and then shoots it out powerfully.
* Upon strong impact, the ball embeds deeply into the rubber surface, transferring explosive energy as it rebounds to create a powerful topspin.
* It is designed to effectively transfer both vertical and horizontal energy simultaneously.

### 3. Overall Recommendation

The **XIOM Jekyll & Hyde X47.5** is recommended for the following players:

1. **Close-to-Table Fast Attackers:** Players who handle the ball with a fast tempo close to the table or enjoy counter-driving after blocking.
2. **Players wanting to boost power:** Those who want to increase distance and speed using the rubber's own repulsion, even with weaker impact.
3. **Backhand Rubber:** Many club players use it for the backhand due to its moderate hardness, good blocking ability, and sharpness in backhand pushes or flicks.

**One-Line Summary:**

> "If you want **faster and more linear aggression** than the V47.5, this is a top choice; however, if you prefer stable connections and loop drives, some adaptation will be necessary."
